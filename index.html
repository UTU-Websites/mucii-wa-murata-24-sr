A little about our services & values

About Us

We specialize in creating customisable short-term event program websites for various events, including funerals, weddings, and business events. Our mission is to provide an interactive, sustainabl alternative to traditional printed programs, reducing environmental impact and offering a seamless digital experiencing.

Why Choose Us

  • Interactive Features: ~ Engage your guest with interactive pages, gallery and graphs.
  • Customization: ~ Tailor your event website to reflect your unique style and requirements with our customisable templates.
    - Background
    - Font
    - Theme colors
    - Gallery layout
    - Language translations
  • Ease of Use: ~ Our user-friendly interface ensures that creating and navigating your event website is simple and stress-free.

Eco-Friendly Initiative

By opting for the Digital Event Program package, you are contributing to a more sustainable environment. This digital platform reduces the need for single-use printed materials, which are often used only for a few hours on the day of the event by minimizing paper waste, the website helps in conserving natural resources and reducing the environmental footprint.

Additional Features

Privacy Settings: Ensure sensitive information and personal messages are protected with customizable privacy settings.
User-Friendly Interface: Design the site with an intuitive, easy-to-navigate interface suitable for users of all ages and technical abilities.
Mobile Compatibility: Optimize the site for access on various devices, ensuring it’s functional on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Eternal Remembrance

A Customizable Memorial/Funeral Website Package

Eternal Remembrance is a comprehensive, customizable funeral website package designed to provide support and information to friends and family during their time of loss. This digital memorial platform offers an array of features to ensure the memory of your loved one is honored and celebrated, providing both practical information and a space for heartfelt tributes. Clients can choose from different pricing tiers based on the pages and features they want included in their package. Here’s a detailed look at what the package includes:

Customizable Packages
Eternal Remembrance offers customizable packages tailored to your needs and budget. Choose from various pricing tiers based on the pages and features you want included:
  • 1. Basic Package: Includes the Home Page, Obituary Information, Wake Schedule, and Contact Information. 30,000 ksh
  • 2. Standard Package: Adds the Program Page, Live Streaming Links, Eulogy Section and Appreciation Page to the Basic Package. 40,000 ksh
  • 3. Premium Package: Includes all features from the Standard Package plus the Interactive Tribute Page, Gallery, and Accessibility Features. 55,000 ksh
  • 4. Heritage Package: Coming soon...

Eternal Remembrance is more than just a website; it’s a digital sanctuary for preserving the memory of your loved one. By offering a blend of essential information, interactive features, and a supportive community space, it helps bring solace and unity during a time of mourning. Choose the package that best suits your needs and let us help you create a lasting tribute to your loved one.
Home Page
Early Notification and Obituary Information
Obituary Information: Share the obituary of your loved one, detailing their life story, achievements, and cherished memories.
Early Notification: Send out early notifications to inform friends and family of the passing before the full site is published.
Basic Details: Include essential details such as date of passing, age, and any immediate service plans.
Wake Schedule and Vigil
Wake Schedule: Clearly outline the schedule of wakes or vigils with precise dates and times.
Location Link: Provide a clickable map link for easy navigation to the wake location.
Contact Information: List phone numbers for point-of-contact individuals who can provide further information.
Donation Links: Integrate links to bank accounts or crowdfunding platforms to raise money for specific causes or funeral expenses.
Program Page
Order of Events: Offer a brief rundown of the events on the day of the funeral, including timings for each segment.
Live Streaming: Include links for live video sharing to enable loved ones abroad to participate virtually.
Eulogy Section: Feature eulogies with interactive options to switch between different languages, ensuring inclusivity for all attendees.
Interactive Hymns: Provide an interactive section for hymns, allowing attendees to follow along with the lyrics and melodies.
Tribute Page
Warm Messages: Family, friends, and colleagues can send their heartfelt messages and condolences via email or WhatsApp for the family to review and approve. Once vetted, these messages will be manually added to the website by the developer.
Interactive Features: Allow users to engage with the tributes by liking, sharing, or commenting, fostering a sense of community and support.
Gallery
Photo Gallery: Host pictures that capture the essence of the departed's life, from childhood memories to recent moments.
Video Gallery: Share videos that highlight significant events and cherished memories, bringing the departed's story to life for attendees.
Accessibility Features: When using a browser, provide brief audio descriptions of pictures and videos to assist those who are visually impaired.
Family Tree
Graphical Family Tree: Display a family tree that helps attendees understand familial relationships, showing who has passed and who is attending.
Speaker Information: Highlight individuals speaking or featured in the program, enhancing connection and understanding among attendees.
Historical Context: Provide a section to honor the family's history, emphasizing the legacy left behind by the departed.
Appreciation Page
Thank You Messages: Allow the family to express gratitude to everyone who contributed, attended, or watched the funeral.
Acknowledgments: Recognize the efforts of individuals or organizations that provided support during the difficult time.
